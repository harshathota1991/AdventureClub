Trip Planning

 Before you head out on a camping trip, it's important to plan carefully. Trip planning includes things such as determine what camping equipment you'll require, figure out what you'll be eating, and prepare for any potential emergencies that may arise. With proper trip planning, you and your group will be sure to have a good time.

Safety Measures

 Camping is a fun way to get family and friends together to enjoy the outdoors. The most effective way to prevent mishaps is to adequately prepare for the trip. Knowledge of the area, weather, terrain, limitations of your body, plus a little common sense can help to ensure a safe and enjoyable trip.
